Code Geass is a Japanese anime television series produced by Sunrise, directed by Gorō Taniguchi and written by Ichirō Ōkouchi. In the year 2010, the Holy Empire of Britannia is establishing itself as a dominant military nation, starting with the conquest of Japan.
